About Tee Stella's

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to luxury African fashion

Our Story

Tee Stella's Fabrics & Stitches was born from a passion for preserving African textile heritage while embracing contemporary luxury fashion. Founded in 2025 by Olutade titilope , a third-generation textile artisan, our brand represents the perfect fusion of traditional craftsmanship and modern elegance.

Growing up in her grandmother's weaving workshop in Lagos, Stella learned the intricate art of African textile creation from an early age. This deep-rooted understanding of fabric, pattern, and technique, combined with formal training at the prestigious Paris Fashion Institute, gave birth to a unique vision: creating luxury fashion that tells the African story with sophistication and global appeal.

Today, Tee Stella's has grown from a small atelier to a renowned luxury fashion house, serving discerning clients across Nigeria and beyond. Our atelier in Lagos employs over 20 master craftspeople, each bringing decades of experience in traditional and contemporary fashion techniques.

Every piece that leaves our atelier carries with it the legacy of African textile artistry, the precision of European tailoring, and the passion of artisans who believe that fashion should be both beautiful and meaningful.

Our Mission

To redefine luxury African fashion by creating exquisite garments that celebrate our rich cultural heritage while meeting the highest international standards of quality and design. We are committed to empowering local artisans, preserving traditional techniques, and providing our clients with fashion that makes them feel confident, elegant, and authentically themselves.

Through sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and continuous innovation, we aim to position African luxury fashion at the forefront of the global fashion industry, one stitch at a time.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most respected luxury fashion house, recognized globally for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and cultural authenticity. We envision a future where African fashion is celebrated not just for its vibrant patterns, but for its sophisticated craftsmanship, sustainable practices, and timeless elegance.

We strive to inspire a new generation of African designers and artisans, creating opportunities for economic growth while preserving the rich textile traditions that have been passed down through generations.
