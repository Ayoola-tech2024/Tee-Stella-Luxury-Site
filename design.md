# Tee Stella's Fabrics & Stitches - Design Language

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Identity
**Luxury Editorial Aesthetic**: Drawing inspiration from high-end fashion magazines like Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Kinfolk. The design embodies sophisticated minimalism with surgical precision in every detail.

### Color Palette
- **Primary Gold**: #D4AF37 - Luxury accent color for highlights, buttons, and premium elements
- **Pure White**: #FFFFFF - Clean backgrounds, generous white space, premium feel
- **Deep Black**: #000000 - Typography, navigation, elegant contrast
- **Soft Gray**: #F8F8F8 - Subtle section backgrounds, card backgrounds
- **Warm Cream**: #FDF9F4 - Alternative background for warmth

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display - Elegant serif for headings, luxury feel
- **Body Font**: Cormorant Garamond - Readable serif for content
- **UI Font**: Poppins - Clean sans-serif for buttons and navigation
- **Accent Font**: Montserrat - Modern sans-serif for labels and metadata

### Visual Language
- **Minimalist Luxury**: Generous white space, clean lines, surgical precision
- **Editorial Quality**: Magazine-style layouts, asymmetric compositions
- **Premium Materials**: Subtle textures, fabric-inspired backgrounds
- **African Elegance**: Cultural authenticity without clichés

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and element animations
- **Splide.js**: Elegant product carousels and testimonial sliders
- **ECharts.js**: Sophisticated data visualization if needed
- **Typed.js**: Dynamic text effects for hero taglines
- **Splitting.js**: Advanced text animations and reveals
- **PIXI.js**: Premium visual effects and backgrounds

### Animation Strategy
- **Subtle Reveal**: Elements fade in with 150-300ms timing
- **Luxury Timing**: Slower, more deliberate animations (vs. tech-savvy speed)
- **Hover Elegance**: Gentle scale transforms, soft shadows
- **Scroll Sophistication**: Progressive disclosure, staggered reveals

### Header Effects
- **Hero Background**: Cinematic fashion photography with subtle parallax
- **Text Animations**: Typewriter effect for tagline with gold accent color
- **Call-to-Action**: Elegant button hover with golden glow

### Interactive Elements
- **Product Cards**: 3D tilt on hover, shadow expansion
- **Navigation**: Smooth underline animations in gold
- **Buttons**: Gentle scale and glow effects
- **Image Galleries**: Ken Burns effect, smooth transitions

### Background Treatment
- **Consistent Foundation**: Soft cream (#FDF9F4) throughout all pages
- **Texture Overlay**: Subtle fabric texture at 3% opacity
- **Section Differentiation**: Gentle geometric shapes in gold tint
- **No Gradients**: Maintaining clean, editorial aesthetic

### Mobile Considerations
- **Touch-Friendly**: 44px minimum touch targets
- **Gesture Support**: Swipe navigation for galleries
- **Responsive Typography**: Fluid scaling for readability
- **Performance**: Optimized animations for mobile devices

### Luxury Details
- **Micro-Interactions**: Button press feedback, form field focus
- **Loading States**: Elegant skeleton screens
- **Error Handling**: Graceful degradation with style
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios, keyboard navigation

## Brand Expression
- **Confidence**: Bold typography, generous spacing
- **Elegance**: Refined color palette, premium materials
- **Authenticity**: Genuine African fashion representation
- **Warmth**: Welcoming copy, approachable luxury
